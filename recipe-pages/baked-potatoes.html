Bombastic Baked Potatoes

Ingredients

  • As many potatoes as you'd like to serve
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Optional

  • 1/2 tbsp butter (per potato)
  • Shredded cheese of your liking
  • Ranch
  • Any other of your favorite toppings

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450F.
  2. Wash off your potatoes very well. Make sure you really scrub that dirt out with your hands.
  3. Stab your potatoes with a fork multiple times on all sides. Take our your anger is needed. This is so they don't literally explode on you so it's kinda important.
  4. While your potatoes are still wet (do not dry them) put some salt onto your hand and srub it all over the potato. This will give it a nice salty crust. You need not be afraid of the sodium. You only live once.
  5. Place your potatoes into the over for 60 minutes or until they reach an internal tempurature of 205F-210F if you want to be fancy.
  6. Once cooked, pull them out of the oven and place them in some form of bakable container. I just a glass dish thingy. Coat them with olive oil but be sure not to drown them it.
  7. Throw them back in the oven for 10 more minutes.
  8. You're done! Plate them and serve. Be sure to cut a slit in them fairly quickly once they're out of the oven so they don't continue to cook.

Notes

Dressing them up with some of the optional ingredients is not necessary but highly recommended if you don't want to look like a weirdo. But if you're chill with being a weirdo then go right ahead, I can't really judge.
