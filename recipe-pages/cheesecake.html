Compliment Worthy Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/4 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 3 pkgs (24oz) of cream cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3 eggs, room temperature
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350F.
  2. Throw some graham crackers into a ziplock bag and crush them up. Personally, I use a rolling pin to beat them up.
  3. Melt your butter and mix it into the graham cracker crumbs.
  4. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9in springform pan. Make sure it's even!
  5. One at a time, beat your creamcheese in either a large bowl or a kitchen aid mixer. Your choice with that.
  6. Throw your sugar in with the cream cheese and beat some more.
  7. One at a time, beat in the eggs. It is very important for it to be one at a time. Scrape down the sides in between each egg.
  8. Add in your vanilla extract and beat one last time.
  9. Add the mixture into the springform pan and bake it for 35-45 minutes or until it's a little bit browned on top. Be sure not to cook it too long.
  10. Let it cool COMPLETELY before serving. It should be left in the fridge for at least 4 hours if not more.
  11. You're done!

Notes

If you make it right you'll get a ton of compliments and be very confused because it's so easy yet so many people are impressed.

People act like it's a crime for your cheesecake to crack but really it's no big deal. It tastes the same in the end. Be proud if you can get it not to crack though!

Please for the love of god serve this with whipped cream, cheesecake is always better with whipped cream.
