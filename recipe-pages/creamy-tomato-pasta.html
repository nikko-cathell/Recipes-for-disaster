Comforting Creamy Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • A pasta of your choice, but prefferably penne
  • 1-2 tbsp of butter
  • 1-2 tbsp of garlic (or more if you're feeling dangerous)
  • 1/2-1 cup of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of heavy cream (or half&half)
  • As much Italian seasoning as you want

Optional

  • Basil. This may be optional but I'm juding you if you don't
  • Oregano
  • Parmesan or mozzarella cheese
  • Chicken bites (find that in its own recipe linked here)

Instructions

  1. Heat up some water and boil your pasta for the appropriate time. Check the packaging to find out what that is.
  2. In the meatime while that's boiling, melt the butter in a skillet. Once melted, saut√© the garlic in the same skillet for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, tomato sauce, cream, and Italian seasoning until it's smooth.
  4. Let the sauce bubble until it's thickened to your liking. Add in your pasta and optional ingredients. You're done!
