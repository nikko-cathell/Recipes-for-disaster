Polite Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Optional

  • Blueberries
  • Chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together all the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar).
  2. Heat up a pan or skillet to medium heat, spray with cooking oil, then scoop the amount of mixture you'd like into the pan/skillet.
  3. Optionally add the blueberries and/or chocolate chips then cook until the edges begin to bubble or the house spirits are tell you they're ready. This is not an exact science but it should be nicely browned.
  4. Flip the pancake over and let it cook for another minute or two.
  5. Plate and serve!

Notes

Obviously you can add whatever toppings you'd like, you are not bound to the chains of the ingredients list.
