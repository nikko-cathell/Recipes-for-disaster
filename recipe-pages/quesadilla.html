Quintessential Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • 1 Tortilla
  • Mexican style shredded cheese (or grated cheese of your choice)
  • Meat of your choice (pre-cooked chicken, carnitas, beef, etc.)
  • 1 Bell pepper cut to your liking

Optional

  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • Avocado
  • Avocado ranch sauce (linked here)
  • Any other dressing of your choice

Instructions

  1. Spray a pan with cooking oil and put your tortilla on it on medium heat.
  2. Place cheese on one half of the tortilla and let melt for a minute or two.
  3. Place your meat and bell peppers on top of the cheese and fold the other half of the tortilla on top of it all.
  4. Carefully flip over the tortilla and cook till both sides are brown and everything is good and melted.
  5. Plate and spread your dressing of choice on top or serve on the side!

Notes

Warning, you will likely have leftover bell peppers and wind up making quesadilas for longer than expected. Get used to eating some quesadillas.
