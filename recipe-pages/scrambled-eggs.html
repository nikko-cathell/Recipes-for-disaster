Super Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Or more eggs
  • A splash of milk
  • Seasonings of your choice
  • 1tbsp of butter
  • Shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Whip your eggs together with the milk and seasonings you've chosen.
  2. Heat a pan to medium high heat and melt the butter in it.
  3. Pour your egg mixture into the pan and as it cooks use a spatula to push the curds into the middle repeatedly.
  4. Cook till they are 90% done then immediately plate with cheese on top. If you cook until they are fully done they will continue to cook while on the plate and become overdone. Don't do that. Trust me.
  5. Enjoy your non-overcooked eggs!

Notes

Warning, you will never see other scrambled eggs the same again.
